The Reverse Career Fair is run by the Information and Computer Science Student Council. (More Information goes here, maybe picture)


Mission
To help students achieve success, enhance the quality of student life, and advance the school’s reputation through our endeavors.

Vision
The Student Council strives to be the center of a highly in demand community of the brightest, most motivated, and talented students in Information and Computer Sciences.

Objectives
We plan to accomplish our mission and vision by:

  • Understanding, disseminating, and contributing to administrative and departmental decisions involving curriculum, research, and personnel actions;
  • Creating a network of alumni which will afford students the best opportunities to grow while in school, and opportunities after graduation to leverage that growth;
  • Providing students with tools and opportunities to become competitive technical experts;
  • Developing nontechnical skills such as leadership, team building, and advocacy which are just as important as technical skills;
  • Leveraging the immense opportunities that are available to them from UCI, the neighboring community, and throughout the world;
  • Creating partnerships between the academics of our ICS School and the Information and Computer Science industries;
  • Displaying the skills, activities, and excitement of the ICS School to the rest of the campus, the nearby community and high schools; and
  • Obtaining funding from alumni, the school, and partnering corporations for student organization activities.
