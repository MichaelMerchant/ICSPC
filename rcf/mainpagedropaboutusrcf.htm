The Reverse Career Fair is run by the Information and Computer Science Student Council.(More Information goes here, maybe picture)


Students in the Bren School for Information and Computer Sciences gather at this event and show off ICS-related projects they have been working on or have finished. Project types include varieties of applications, ranging from phones to video games.
